A Conversation About Your Experience

We're glad you're here. Before we begin, please take a moment to read through the information below — it'll only take a minute.

Before we begin

What this is about

This is a space to share something that's been on your mind — a personal experience you'd like to talk through with an AI companion. As part of an academic research project, we're exploring how people experience these conversations.

Here's what to expect

  1. A short screening questionnaire (approximately 5–8 minutes)
  2. A brief introduction and setup for the voice conversation
  3. A voice-based conversation with an AI partner on an assigned topic (approximately 10–15 minutes). We recommend using headphones and finding a quiet space beforehand.
  4. A short post-conversation questionnaire of approximately 15 questions (approximately 3–5 minutes)

Time

The whole thing takes about 20–30 minutes, at your own pace.

A few things to know

The conversation topics involve personal emotional experiences, which some people find mildly uncomfortable. You're free to stop at any time without penalty. There are no right or wrong answers — we're simply interested in your honest experience.

Your privacy

Your responses are kept strictly confidential. Your Prolific ID is used only to link your data within this study and will not be shared publicly. All data is stored securely and used for academic research only.

Voice recording

This study uses voice-based conversation. Your microphone will be used to record your spoken responses. Each recording will be automatically transcribed, and both the audio file and the transcript will be stored securely on our research servers. By participating, you consent to this voice recording and storage.

Voluntary participation

Taking part is entirely your choice. You may withdraw at any point by closing your browser, though you will not receive compensation if the study is left incomplete.

Questions?

If anything is unclear, please reach out to the research team via Prolific messaging.
